Welcome to my Node.js Web app.


This app holds two functionalities:

  • Factorial/Summation Calculator
  • Seven Days Weather Forecast

Click on the Menu on the top left to view the links. You can then click on the desired menu item and you will be redirected to the respective template.

On the calculator page, you enter the valid input and choose on the dropdown menu for factorial/summation.
After this you click on the Calculate button and if the inputs are correct, you should be see the results.
Likewise, if you select the Weather menu, you will be redirected to the weather page.
Overthere, when you click the 'Get Forecast' button you should be able to view a table that displays the forecast for the recent seven days.
Lastly, when you click on the Home menu you should be redirected to this page.
